## Path B — federal PPDR (self-pay bill exceeds Good Faith Estimate by $400+)

Not a letter — a filing. Requirements and process:

- Deadline: within **120 calendar days** of the bill date.
- File at cms.gov/medical-bill-rights (Patient-Provider Dispute Resolution),
  $25 administrative fee (recouped via a $25 bill reduction if you prevail).
- Attach: the Good Faith Estimate and the bill exceeding it by $400+.
- While the dispute is pending, the provider cannot send the bill to
  collections and cannot charge late fees.

The agent prepares the packet (GFE + bill + one-paragraph statement of the
discrepancy); the user files and pays the $25 — the agent never submits
government forms on the user's behalf.
